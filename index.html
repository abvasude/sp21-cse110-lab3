List of Entries for the last 3 days

In this page, I will explain what I have done in the last three days in order starting from Wednesday, April 7th.

Wednesday Morning

On Wednesday morning, I woke up in the morning around 7:30 am and prepared to go get my first vaccine. I quickly ate breakfast and around 8 am, my mom and I headed off to Pittsburg, California to get my Covid-19 vaccine.

Appointment

After a 45 minute drive, I finally arrived at the health center in which I had to wait for 25 minutes because of the long line. After around 35 people moved forward, I finally was able to enter. Then, I was asked to provide my personal information and once that was done, I entered the room where there were 12 stations set up for the vaccine to be distributed. I waited for a minute or so and then they quickly asked some more information before injecting me with the vaccine.

Observation

Soon after I was placed in an observation room for 15 minutes where I read about the possible side effects of the Pfeizer vaccine and how effective it is. During the 7 minute mark, one lady started screaming at the

top of her lungs and everyone jumped from their seats.
I thought to myself, Everything is fine. It was probably an allergic reaction to the vaccine. I probably do not have to worry. As it turns out, the lady was just on the phone and started yelling at someone at the top of her lungs in a foreign language. After that happened, I spent the last 5 minutes on my phone when I was texting my mom about when she should pick me up.

After a 50 minute car ride where my mom kept asking me what happened, if I feel okay, or if I need to sleep, we finally arrived back home where I attended my classes.

Wednesday Afternoon

Nothing truly exciting happened this afternoon. My arm was still in pain from the vaccine but I ate lunch (pasta with some steamed vegetables). I talked to my parents for some time and I informed them time and time again that I am fine and I do not feel any of the major symptoms. After lunch, I had to watch the recording of the 9 AM class which I had missed.

Wednesday Evening

I had chosen to rest for the remainder of the evening instead of starting on the CSE 110 Lab because I had started to feel a little shortness in breath which is one of the side effects of the vaccine. As a result, I had a very light dinner (tomato soup with croutons) and immediately went to bed. I woke up in the middle of the night feeling hungry so I went downstairs and it was there that I felt a bit lightheaded. I neededto eat something or at least drink water to make sure that I wouldn't pass out. After sitting down for a bit and drinking some water, I felt better and went back to bed.

Thursday

My Thursday Morning was a typical day where I have 2 back to back classes from 9:30 to 12:20. After attending both of my classes, I ate lunch but I was too tired because of the vaccine. So, I ended up napping for 2 hours before having to attend my 4 PM Software Engineering class. Here we learned about the project and more information about what we needed to do in order to succeed. After that class, I remembered that I had to do a lab for my ECE 100 class that was due the next day so I quickly got to work on that. After coding for a few hours on Matlab, I finally finished my report and submitted. When I am feeling stressed out, I like to watch videos of nature such as this one of the Earth.

In between, around 7 PM, I ate dinner and around 9 PM, once I finished the report, I went to bed.

Friday

Friday Morning

Friday was definitely an interesting day to say the least. When I got up around 8 AM, I was surprised to see my parents watching cricket downstairs. It was then that I remembered that this was the first day of the IPL and the match was Royal Challengers Bangalore vs. the Mumbai Indians. Knowing that these teams had a huge rivalry, I knew it would be a great game to watch. However, when I came downstairs, I realized that the match already started and it was almost time to switch the batting and the bowling team. So, I asked my parents to give me a rundown of the important highlights from the first half. I asked them, "Who's batting?"
My dad said, "Mumbai Indians".
Then, I asked, "Did any bowler or batsman stand out?"
My mom replied, "Yes, one RCB bowler got 5 wickets in the game"
I replied with, "Wow".
